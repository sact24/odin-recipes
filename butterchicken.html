Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. In addition sometimes the cream gives the curry sauce a silky smooth rich texture.

It originated in the Indian subcontinent as a curry.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato Puree
  • butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Onion
  • Indian Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade and marinate chicken overnight
  2. Prepare the masala and then blend to make puree for the gravy
  3. Cook marinated chicken in the gravy
  4. Garnish Butter Chicken with cream and serve hot
